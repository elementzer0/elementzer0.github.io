Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Pregnancy Journal is a Google Sheets template created for personal pregnancy tracking. This Privacy Policy explains how the template's built-in script handles your data.

What data is accessed

The script accesses only the Google Sheets spreadsheet in which it operates. It does not access any other files, folders, or Google account data.

What data is collected

No data is collected, stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party. All information you enter remains solely in your own Google Sheets file.

External content

This template loads decorative images hosted on GitHub. No personal data is sent to or collected by GitHub in this process.

Google OAuth permissions

When you first open the template, Google will ask you to authorize the script. This permission is required for the script's automation features to function. You can revoke this access at any time via your Google Account security settings.
